Winner Eleven’s Public Blockchain Architecture

The Winner Eleven Platform aims to use blockchain technology to provide open platform services for decentralized sports ecosystem chain, giving all business participants transparent application development services and business realizations through operation of blockchain ecosystem chain and tokenization of assets in order to achieve business goals. The Winner Eleven Sports Guessing Game includes sporting contests, online community, its promoting platform, sports guessing/prediction platform, and the living community to communicate with favored sports clubs and teams, other token holders and investors.

The Consensus Algorithm

Winner Eleven‘s application framework provides a rich set of application protocols and modules that enable third-party DApp developers to quickly build decentralized applications without having to understand the complexities of the underlying distributed ledger. Winner Eleven's application framework has a high degree of scalability and can be expanded according to scenarios' needs. Most of sports events/tournaments are happened sequentially so that the DApp will incur a high sequential visiting volume but still require to maintain a constant information flow rate.

The Sports Guessing Platform

The Winner Eleven Sports Guessing Game platform offers an open-source blockchain ecosystem platform to record all game players’ guessing times, guessed sports contests and results, interactions with favored sports teams in the community as well as participants‘ transactions in different scenarios, such as gaming and entertainment by the use of decentralized account, the bankability of certain WE token holder’s account in the ethereum wallet as well as other associated transactions in-between ethereum-based tokens. WE‘s three-layer algorithm consensus enables effective integration of game players’ guessing experience and transparency in earning WE coins and eventual rewards given back protocol.
